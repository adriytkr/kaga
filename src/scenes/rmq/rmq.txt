range minimum query: finding the minimum value in any subarray

Rows → starting index
Columns → length as power of two

This cell corresponds to the subarray that starts at index 3 and has a length of 2² = 4.

The minimum value is -3,

so we store it.

To find the minimum of a large subarray

we halve it into two previously computed subarrays.

We have already calculated their minimum.

Take the minimum between them.

Any query can be computed by just looking at the table.

If the length is not a power of 2, take the largest power of 2 that fits.

Query from the left

and query from the right.

Together, they cover the whole subarray.

The minimum between them is the minimum value.
