  ## What is Bismarck about?

  (Just for the sake of curiosity, **Bismarck** stands for ???)

  Most classes, articles and videos **rely on memorization** as their primary instrument for teaching, and I think that is a huge mistake for two main reasons:

  1. You have no clue **why** are you are doing what you are doing.
  2. It becomes exauhsting almost immediately.

  My primary goal is to show the beauty and hidden joy of Math, Physics and Coding through **active learning**.

  That is why I built this blog. If you happen to share a similar opinion, you won't wanna miss this blog.

  # About me

  I'm from Brazil and here I notice that most schools and even universities promote a method of studying that prioritizes memorization. Students passively learn the topics and get good grades, but they are just mechanically following step without a true grasp of the underlying logic.
  
  At the beginning, I didn't really care; I thought this was the proper way of studying and there was no other way around. However, when I began studying by myself, I realized that any topic became easier — and much more enjoyable — when I was able to visualize them and understand how things work **under the hood**.
