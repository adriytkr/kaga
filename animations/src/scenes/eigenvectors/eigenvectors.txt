# ---------------- Apply Linear Transformation ----------------
Scaled by a factor of 2

But its direction remain unchanged.

This is what we call eigenvector.

The factor of 2 is called eigenvalue.



# ---------------- Eigenvector Family ----------------
Any vector on the span of an eigenvector is also an eigenvector.



# ---------------- Example ----------------
The is also an eigenvector

It still remains on its span even after the transformation

The eigenvalue associated with it is -1



# ---------------- Matrix with zero eigenvectors ----------------
Not every matrix has eigenvectors.

Take the matrix that represents 30 degrees rotation.

Every vector changes direction.

So the matrix has zero eigenvectors.
